Herbie Hancock - Cantaloupe Island

Cantaloupe Island is a Jazz standard that was recorded by Herbie Hancock in 1964. It was within his album Empyrean Isles, when Herbie Hancock was still part of the Miles Davis quintet. Hancock was on piano, Freddie Hubbard on cornet, Ron carter on Bass, and Tony Williams on drums. Be sure to give this classic Jazz standard a listen as it brings you through the funky and cool rhythms of Herbie Hancock's genius.
